    Communication is a Skill….and an essentiality.
      Subham Banerjee     29-July-2017     Like     Tag     Comment  
    There was a time when a certain degree alone could land a person in his/her coveted job. Not anymore. There has been a paradigm shift in the essential skills required to get a job and more importantly sustain it. In the present era, one cannot deny & ignore the importance of communication skills to make a mark in the professional world. Before dwelling on the importance of communication skills, let us understand why communication is called a skill. Skill as we know is defined as something we can do - like swimming or cycling or playing the guitar. A lot of people can talk, and they talk in various languages. However, do all know the art of speaking? Communication is not just talking. Communication is the art of speaking. It is about knowing what to say, when to say and more importantly how to say. Communication is about listening intently and then speaking what must be spoken. Listening is a key ingredient for excelling in communication skills. Only a good listener can be a good communicator.Verbal communication is not just about what we say but a crux of it lies in the way we say. In case of verbal communication, style has more impact than content.
